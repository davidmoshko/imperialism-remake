What is Vorbis Support?

Ogg/Vorbis is a free audio format featuring high compression ratios. It is widely used and there are libraries who enable support for Ogg in Java. JOrbis by JCraft is a pure Java Ogg/Vorbis decoder and Ogg Vorbis SPI by JavaZoom registers JOrbis as a service for the . The later also relies partly on the Tritonus library (shared plugin).
